The Terra-Balance Story

At Terra Balance Enterprises, we are more than just a business. We are a movement transforming African agriculture through sustainability, innovation, and empowerment. By tackling key issues such as waste management, unemployment, and accessibility to investment, we create long-term impact and sustainable opportunities for communities.

So where did this idea come from?

Our journey began with a simple yet powerful vision—empowering smallholder farmers and revolutionizing sustainable farming in Africa. With deep roots in agriculture, our founders saw the need for an innovative solution that not only supports farmers but also drives economic growth, fosters job creation, and ensures food security.

Secondly, the food industry in Nigeria is facing significant challenges. Local food producers are grappling with a variety of pressures, including rising costs due to inflation, disruptions in supply chains, competition from imported goods, and squeezed margins from larger retail chains. Selling directly to consumers (DTC) through online platforms offers food producers a higher-margin opportunity to generate revenue. However, setting up and managing an online store—including branding, website development, marketing, and ongoing management—can be overwhelming, diverting attention from their primary business activities.

That’s where Terra Balance comes in

We bridge the gap between sustainable farming and economic growth by:

  • Transforming waste into valuable resources through recycling and eco-friendly methods.
  • Creating jobs and dignified work opportunities for youth.
  • Offering micro-investment options to low-income earners.
  • Closing the education gap through knowledge-sharing and training.

Our Story

Founded on January 9th, 2023, Terra Balance Enterprises started with a clear mission: to build a sustainable agricultural enterprise that benefits both farmers and the economy. We began with 5 acres of farmland in Ile-Ife, Osun State, and expanded to an additional 2 acres in Ikire.

Guided by the vision of our founders, Omikunle Timileyin Michael and Ogunfile Abraham Tosin, we continue to push the boundaries of what sustainable agriculture can achieve.
